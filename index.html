The Foundation
1 Samuel 22:1–2
“

So David left Gath and escaped to the cave of Adullam. Soon his brothers and all his other relatives joined him there.

Then others began coming—men who were in trouble or in debt or who were just discontented—until David was the captain of about 400 men.

”
New Living Translation (NLT)

David did not wait for perfect men. He gathered the broken, the burdened, and the restless. He put God in the middle of it. And out of that cave came something powerful. That is exactly what this is.

Who We Are

Built on Three Things

01
God in It

Everything we do starts and ends with faith. This is not a club. It is a brotherhood with a purpose higher than any one of us.

02
No Judgment

Come as you are. No performance. No posturing. Just men in the room being real with each other and building something together.

03
Give Back

Mentorship. Service. Building homes. Showing up for kids. We are not just here for ourselves. We are here for the community around us.
